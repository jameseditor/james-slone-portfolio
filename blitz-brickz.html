Blitz Brickz - Lego Channel

Before client work, before fancy gear — it was just me, LEGO, my phone, and an idea.

Blitz Brickz was a channel where I scripted, filmed, and edited over 100 videos entirely on my own. Focused on Marvel, DC, and comedy, it became my personal editing lab. Every upload taught me something new about storytelling, timing, and making content that connects with people. Looking back, it’s still one of the projects I’m most proud of.

The First Ever Marvel Sets

A commentary video exploring the very first LEGO Marvel sets released in 2002. Packed with skits, jokes, and nostalgic insights.

Cancelled LEGO Superhero Sets

This deep dive uncovers LEGO sets that were planned but never released. From concept art to scrapped prototypes, it’s a look into the "what could have been" of the LEGO superhero lineup.

The Final Video

The final upload on the Blitz Brickz channel. In this video, I explored some of the wildest fake LEGO leaks that made waves in the community but turned out to be completely false. It was a fun way to wrap things up. While this channel is now closed, it marked the beginning of my journey and everything I learned here now fuels my new channel focused on editing and storytelling.
