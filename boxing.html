Boxing Videos

Topic Fighter

This is the video that reinforced in my mind how important storytelling is in editing. Many times, opening this video, I kept wanting to add effects and transitions. But to me, not having them made the story more impactful. I focused on grabbing powerful audio lines from the seminar and contrasting them with footage of his actual fights. I wanted to make you truly feel the impact and understand what the boxer went through to get to where he is now.
