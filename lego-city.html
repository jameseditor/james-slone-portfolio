How Much LEGO Can You Buy for $100?

Budget videos are always super relatable and this one’s all about how much LEGO you can get for $100. Generic Stud cracked a lot of jokes and found some crazy good deals, and I made sure the editing backed that up. Throughout the video, I focused on clarity and entertainment: on-screen prices with fun sound effects, meme cuts, B-roll, and transitions that keep the viewer engaged without overwhelming the story. The goal? Make it easy to follow, fun to watch, and visually clean.
