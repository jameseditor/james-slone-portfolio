Recreating Marvel Rivals Characters in Lego

I had the opportunity to edit a video fully sponsored by Marvel, working with Republic Studs, a LEGO YouTuber I’ve collaborated with for years. The goal was to recreate every Marvel Rivals character in LEGO, all while carefully following Marvel’s branding guidelines.

With minimal revisions from Marvel and seamless sponsor integration, the result is a fun, fast-paced video that blends creative builds with sponsor messaging.

What is Marvel Rivals?

Marvel Rivals is a fast-growing third-person hero shooter featuring iconic Marvel heroes and villains. Editing an officially sponsored video tied to a game of this scale was a major milestone it pushed me creatively, tested my attention to brand detail, and gave me a chance to contribute to something fans truly care about. I'm proud to feature it in my portfolio.
