About Me

Resume CV

I’m James, a video editor with over 5 years of experience. My work has helped creators reach over 80 million views across platforms. I've collaborated with top YouTubers and built my own channel, so I understand the creative process from both sides.

I’m driven by growth and storytelling. My focus is on edits that feel purposeful—from rhythm and pacing to structure and emotion. I don’t just make videos look good—I make them connect.
